# Employee Recruitment Management System

> Developed by **Dominic, Ece, Furkan & Keethan** FHNW Projekt 2026

## About
This project models and implements the **employee recruitment process** 
of a company – one of the most strategic workflows in any organization.

## Process Overview
1. **Job Definition & Advertising** – Posting and promoting open positions
2. **Candidate Identification** – Screening and shortlisting applicants
3. **Interview Process** – Structured and unstructured interview workflows
4. **Evaluation & Selection** – Collaborative candidate assessment
5. **Hiring & Contract Negotiation** – Offer management and negotiation rounds
6. **Onboarding** – Integrating new hires into the company

## Key Features
- Supports **structured** and **unstructured** recruitment activities
- Enables **team collaboration** and **stakeholder decision-making**
- Models real-world hiring scenarios end-to-end
